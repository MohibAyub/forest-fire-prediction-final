Analyse Forest Fire Risk

Use the moveable marker on the forest map to get latitude & longitude values.

This webpage displays the moveable map of Montesinho Natural Park, including a marker. The marker can also be moved around the map.

How this works?

The draggble marker on the forest map when dropped at a given location receives the coordinates of the location as latitude & longitude pairs. These latitude and longitude values are used by  openweathermap  API to obtain real-time weather attributes (Temperature, RH, Wind, Rain) of the given coordinates. The weather attributes are used to evaluate various indices of the Fire Weather Index (FWI) (FFMC, DMC, DC, ISI etc.) and the risk of fire is predicted on the basis of these indices given as inputs to a deep neural network model.
